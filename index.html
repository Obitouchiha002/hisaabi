01 Play Store pe kyun nahi hai?

Do wajah hain. Ek — notification access maangne wali apps ka store review mahino chal jata hai, aur usme product ki raftaar maar khati hai. Do — store ke bahar rehne se har update seedha tum tak aata hai, kisi review ka intezaar nahi.

APK GitHub Releases pe rehti hai, aur app khud update check kar leti hai. Aage user badhne pe Play Store pe bhi aa sakti hai.

02 Kya ye mere bank ka data padhta hai?

Nahi. Bank se koi connection nahi, net-banking login nahi, account number kahin nahi maanga jata. App sirf tumhare phone pe aane wale notification ka text dekhti hai — jaise "₹240 debited" — aur usme se sirf amount aur dukaan ka naam nikalti hai.

Ye poora kaam phone ke andar hota hai. Notification ka text kisi server pe, kisi AI ko, kahin nahi jata.
03 SMS permission maangoge kya?

Bilkul nahi. Bank ka SMS aata hai to uska notification bhi messages app se dikh jata hai — wahi se kaam ban jata hai. Isliye READ_SMS permission ki zaroorat hi nahi padti.

Ye jaan-boojh kar liya gaya faisla hai: jitni kam permission, utna kam bharosa maangna padta hai.

04 AI galat entry bana de to?

Koi bhi AI ya auto entry seedha save nahi hoti. Pehle ek chhota confirm card dikhta hai — amount, category, dukaan — jise ek tap me badal ya hata sakte ho.

Galat hisaab se to hisaab na hona hi behtar hai. Isliye chupke se kuch add nahi kiya jata.

05 Internet ke bina chalega?

Haan. Entries likhna, reports dekhna, auto-capture — sab offline chalta hai, kyunki data pehle phone me save hota hai.

Internet sirf do jagah chahiye: voice note ko samajhne ke liye, aur doosre device pe sync karne ke liye. Net wapas aate hi sab apne aap sync ho jata hai.

06 Hindi me bolun ya English me?

Jaise bolte ho waise bolo. "Chai twenty", "chai bees rupaye", "chai 20" — teeno chalega. Mixed Hinglish bhi samajh aata hai.

Number bolne ka tarika bhi koi bhi ho — "do sau", "dhai sau", "sava sau" sab handle hote hain.

07 iPhone pe chalega?

Web app iPhone pe chalta hai — voice entry, manual entry, poori reports sab. Bas auto-capture iOS pe possible hi nahi hai, kyunki Apple kisi app ko doosri app ke notifications padhne nahi deta.

Isliye Android hi main platform rahega.

08 Mera data export ho sakta hai?

Haan, kabhi bhi — CSV, JSON ya PDF. Data tumhara hai; app chhodni ho to poora leke jao, koi rok-tok nahi.

Account delete karo to server se bhi sab hat jata hai.

09 Kya ye sach me free hai?

Manual entry aur auto-capture hamesha free rahenge — wahi roz ki aadat banate hain, unpe paisa maangna galat lagta hai.

Paisa sirf AI wale bhaari kaam pe lagta hai (unlimited voice, sawaal-jawaab, receipt scan) kyunki unka asli kharcha hai. Early access me wo bhi free hai.
