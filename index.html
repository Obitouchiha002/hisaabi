FAQ

Jo sabse zyada poocha jata hai.

Play Store pe kyun nahi hai?
Notification-access wali apps ka store review lamba chalta hai aur usme product ki speed maar khati hai. Filhal APK seedha GitHub Releases se milta hai — app khud hi update check kar leti hai. Aage jaake Play Store pe bhi aa sakta hai.
Kya ye mere bank ka data padhta hai?
Nahi. Bank se koi connection nahi, koi net-banking login nahi. App sirf tumhare phone pe aane wale notifications ka text dekhti hai — wo bhi phone ke andar hi process hota hai. Kisi server pe kuch nahi bheja jata.
SMS permission maangoge kya?
Bilkul nahi. Bank ke SMS ka notification bhi messages app se hi mil jata hai, isliye READ_SMS permission ki zaroorat hi nahi padti.
Internet ke bina chalega?
Haan. Entries, reports, auto-capture — sab offline chalta hai. Sirf voice note ko samajhne wale AI ke liye internet chahiye; net aane pe sab sync ho jata hai.
iPhone pe?
Web app iPhone pe bhi chalta hai (voice + manual entry, poori reports). Lekin auto-capture iOS pe technically possible hi nahi hai — wo Android-only feature rahega.
AI galat entry bana de to?
Isliye koi bhi AI ya auto entry seedha save nahi hoti — pehle ek chhoti si confirm card dikhti hai jise ek tap me edit ya delete kar sakte ho. Galat hisaab se koi hisaab na hona behtar hai.
Mera data export kar sakta hoon?
Haan, kabhi bhi — CSV, JSON ya PDF. Data tumhara hai, app chhodni ho to poora leke jao.
